PROJECT CATALOGUE

IPFO Q2 2025 Project Catalogue

Purpose of the Catalogue

As part of Indonesia's effort to accelerate infrastructure investment, the Indonesia Project Facilitation Office (IPFO) has prepared a Project Catalogue featuring a list of infrastructure projects across various sectors, each with its respective level of readiness.

The catalogue was first introduced at the International Conference on Infrastructure (ICI) 2025 as a demonstration of the Government's commitment to transparency and openness in presenting a clear project pipeline. Through IPFO, this catalogue now serves as the official portal for investors, development partners, and stakeholders to access information on Indonesia's infrastructure projects.

Contents of the Catalogue

The catalogue presents:

  • A list of infrastructure projects at various stages of development, with a focus on those that are available for private investment.
  • Detailed information for each project, including location, structure, financial metrics, investment scheme, status, government support mechanism, and project timelines.
  • Key contact persons from the proposing institutions, enabling direct engagement.
  • Opportunities to explore both solicited and unsolicited projects of national strategic importance.

Intended Audience

This catalogue is designed for:

  • Investors and financial institutions seeking credible, well-prepared infrastructure pipelines.
  • Private sector developers, operators, and technical consultants interested in partnership opportunities.
  • Policymakers and public officials involved in infrastructure planning, financing, and delivery.
  • Conference delegates attending thematic sessions, exhibitions, or one-on-one meetings.
